# Business Insights

## Customer Activity
The database includes customers across several states and customer types, including Retail, Online, VIP, Wholesale, and Business customers. Reviewing customer activity can help identify which customer groups place the largest orders and generate the most paid revenue.

## Product Performance
The product catalog includes Electronics, Furniture, Accessories, and Office Supplies. Revenue analysis uses product price and order quantity to identify which products contribute the most sales value.

## Order Workflow
Orders are recorded in several stages, including Completed, Pending, Shipped, Processing, Cancelled, and Refunded. Pending and processing orders may require follow-up to confirm payment status, shipment progress, or customer communication.

## Payment Review
Payments include Paid, Incomplete, Cancelled, and Refund statuses. Incomplete payments connected to pending orders should be reviewed because they may represent unfinished transactions or delayed payment collection.

## Reporting Value
This project shows how SQL can connect customer, product, order, and payment data to support sales reporting, revenue analysis, and operational decision-making.
